Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our engineering services

What engineering services does SEBA provide?

+

SEBA Engineering provides comprehensive structural engineering services including:

  • Structural design for steel and concrete structures
  • Steel manufacturing with quality control
  • Site erection services
  • Engineering consultancy

We handle projects from initial concept through detailed design and construction, offering end-to-end solutions for structural needs.

Does SEBA work on international projects?

+

Yes, SEBA Engineering works on projects globally. We have experience with international structural codes and standards including:

  • Eurocodes
  • ASCE/SEI standards
  • Various national building codes

Our team can deliver compliant designs regardless of project location, and we regularly collaborate with local partners to ensure seamless project execution.

What industries does SEBA typically work with?

+

SEBA Engineering works across multiple sectors including:

  • Industrial (manufacturing facilities, warehouses, process plants)
  • Commercial (office buildings, retail spaces)
  • Agricultural (storage facilities, processing structures)
  • Municipal (public buildings, infrastructure)

We have particular expertise in facilities requiring specialized engineering solutions and performance-critical structures.

How does SEBA approach steel versus concrete design decisions?

+

We evaluate multiple factors including:

  • Project requirements and performance criteria
  • Construction timeline
  • Budget constraints
  • Site conditions and accessibility
  • Long-term maintenance considerations
  • Sustainability goals

Our engineers analyze both steel and concrete options where appropriate, presenting clients with data-driven comparisons to make informed decisions about the optimal structural system for their specific needs.

What software and tools does SEBA use for structural analysis and design?

+

SEBA Engineering utilizes industry-leading software including:

  • Advanced finite element analysis programs
  • Building Information Modeling (BIM) solutions
  • Specialized steel and concrete design software
  • Custom calculation tools for specific applications

Our approach combines advanced computational methods with practical engineering judgment to deliver optimized designs that are both safe and efficient.

How does SEBA ensure quality control in manufacturing?

+

Our quality control system includes:

  • Material certification verification
  • Dimensional checks at multiple stages
  • Welding procedure qualification and testing
  • Non-destructive testing where required
  • Surface preparation inspection
  • Final assembly verification

We implement rigorous inspection protocols at key stages of fabrication to ensure components meet specifications before shipping to site.

How does SEBA handle site safety during erection?

+

Safety is paramount in our site operations. Our approach includes:

  • Comprehensive site-specific safety plans
  • Regular toolbox talks and safety briefings
  • Implementation of appropriate fall protection systems
  • Proper equipment maintenance and inspection
  • Ensuring all personnel are properly trained and equipped
  • Clear emergency response procedures

Our erection procedures are designed with safety as the primary consideration, and we maintain strict compliance with all regulatory requirements.

What is SEBA's approach to sustainable design?

+

We integrate sustainability principles throughout our design process, focusing on:

  • Material efficiency and optimization
  • Selection of structural systems that minimize environmental impact
  • Designs that accommodate future adaptability
  • Specification of materials with recycled content where appropriate
  • Design for disassembly and material reuse
  • Reduction of embodied carbon through intelligent design

We can help projects achieve various green building certifications through intelligent structural solutions that balance performance, cost, and environmental considerations.

How does the engineering consultation process work?

+

Our consultation process follows these general steps:

  1. Initial discussion to understand project requirements and objectives
  2. Proposal development outlining scope, timeline, and deliverables
  3. Concept development and preliminary analysis
  4. Detailed design development with regular client interaction
  5. Production of comprehensive design documentation
  6. Implementation support during construction

We emphasize clear communication throughout the process, ensuring clients are fully informed and involved in key decisions affecting their project.

Can SEBA help with existing structure evaluations and modifications?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive assessment services for existing structures, including:

  • Condition surveys and documentation
  • Structural capacity evaluations
  • Retrofit and strengthening designs
  • Adaptive reuse engineering
  • Resolution of structural issues and deficiencies
  • Extension and modification designs

Whether you need to adapt a structure for new use or address structural concerns, our team can develop appropriate engineering solutions tailored to your specific situation.
